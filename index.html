I have been a video game lover since my childhood. When I discovered I could study and dedicate myself to it, I haven't stopped pursuing it.

About Me

I started by doing a higher-level course in 3D animation and I am currently finishing my degree in video game development. When I began, what I enjoyed most was designing and animating, until I discovered the producer role. Since I was a child, I have loved organizing and leading work teams, and in all my subject projects or game jams, I have taken on this role. The project where I worked the most as a producer was Warhammer 40,000: The Last Marine, where I was the producer of a team of 40 people.

Projects

Warhammer 40,000: The Last Marine

The most ambitious project I've worked on: a Diablo-like shooter set in the Warhammer 40K universe. I led a team of 40 people as the full-time producer over the course of 4 months.

Süreg's Mask

The project I’m most emotionally attached to. A story-driven RPG developed during the “Project 2” course, where I coordinated a team of 20 people as the producer and also contributed to the game design. It was awarded and showcased at IndieDev Day 2024.

Viktor Stonehart

My first project: a psychological horror game with puzzle mechanics, developed during the CITM Game Jam. I worked as the lead designer in a team of 5 people.

Hired or Fired

An innovative VR game where you must interrogate potential fraudsters using your own voice via microphone. Developed by a 7-person team. I was the producer, main designer and QA tester.
